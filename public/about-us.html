A Little About us?

  • Who we Are : We are a purpose driven, awareness creating organization to help and serve the lives in need of blood, organs and to donate bodies for research
  • What we Do : We Collaborate with Healthcare professionals, Hospitals, Blood Banks, Government, NGO’s, Corporates and local communities and play our part in strengthening Healthcare system.

    • At Happy Donors, we will connect and serve.

  • Blood Donors: Our strong donor community will be in reach at the time of need of blood
  • Blood Requestor: We process every blood request as someone in our family is in need with utmost care, urgency, and respect.
  • Organ-Body Donation: We educate, create awareness about Organ-Body Donation and connect with Government registries to Hospitals and requestors
  • NGO’s and Local Communities: Our core strength is our NGO’s and local communities where we arrange the blood donation/medical camps.
