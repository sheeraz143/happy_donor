Refund Policy

Home / Refund Policy

At Happy Donors, we are committed to maintaining the trust and support of our generous donors, supporters, and event participants. We value your contributions and aim to provide transparency regarding our Return & Refund Policy.

1. Donations
  • We deeply appreciate the generosity of all donors. Please note that, as is standard practice in the nonprofit sector, donations made to Happy Donors are generally non-refundable. We recommend that all donors carefully consider their contribution before making a donation.
  • However, in the rare instance of an error in processing or if a donation was made in error (e.g., duplicate transaction or incorrect amount), please contact us within 2 days of the transaction date. We will review the situation on a case-by-case basis and determine if a refund is warranted.
2. Refund Process

If your return or refund request is approved, the process will typically be as follows:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method (credit card, bank transfer, etc.), unless otherwise agreed upon.
  • Refunds for donations may take up to 7-14 working days to process.
3. Exemptions

Refunds or returns are not possible under the following circumstances:

  • Donations made in support of general operations or specific campaigns.
  • In cases where the donation or purchase was made knowingly and voluntarily (e.g., in exchange for event attendance or a charitable pledge).
4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your donation, purchase, or return request, please reach out to us at info@happydonors.ngo

We are committed to resolving any issues and ensuring that your experience with Happy Donors is positive and transparent.
